# Description
[Bloons Tower Defense](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bloons_TD_1) is the game that revolutionized the web-based tower defense genre. Instead of solving puzzles, your goal here is to stop waves of balloons from reaching the end of the track. Strategically place Dart Monkeys, Tack Towers, and Bomb Shooters to pop the bloons before they escape.

# Controls
Mouse - Select and place towers.

Click - Upgrade towers or sell them.
