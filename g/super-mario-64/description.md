# Description
[Super Mario 64](https://www.mariowiki.com/Super_Mario_64) is a groundbreaking 3D platformer developed by [Nintendo EAD](https://www.mariowiki.com/Nintendo_Entertainment_Analysis_and_Development) and released in 1996 for the [Nintendo 64](https://www.mariowiki.com/Nintendo_64). As the first 3D entry in the iconic [Super Mario](https://www.mariowiki.com/Super_Mario) series, players control Mario as he explores Princess Peach's castle and jumps into magical paintings to collect Power Stars and rescue the princess from Bowser. Featuring revolutionary 360-degree analog movement, innovative camera controls, and 15 unique worlds filled with secrets, Super Mario 64 defined the 3D platforming genre and remains one of the most influential video games ever created.

# Controls
Left Click - Grab objects (when close).

WASD / Arrow Keys - Move Mario.

Space - Jump; wall kick (press near wall while jumping).

Shift - Crouch; crawl; trigger special jumps.

Z - Ground pound (press while airborne).

X - Punch; kick; dive (while running).

C - Read signs; talk to NPCs; interact.

F - Toggle camera mode (Lakitu/Mario).

Arrow Keys / Mouse - Adjust camera angle.

Escape - Pause menu.
