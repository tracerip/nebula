# Description
Minecraft 1.12.2 is a highly stable release of the iconic sandbox survival game developed by Mojang Studios. Known as "The World of Color Update," this version introduced concrete blocks, glazed terracotta, parrots, the recipe book, advancements system, and illusioners. Version 1.12.2 remains the gold standard for modded Minecraft due to its exceptional stability and massive mod library, making it the preferred choice for modpacks like RLCraft, SkyFactory, and countless others.

# Controls
Left Click - Attack/mine blocks; pick up all items from a slot; split a stack evenly (hold and drag); sort loose items into one stack (double click).
Right Click - Place blocks/use items; pick up half of a stack; place one item from a stack (hold and drag).
WASD - Move forward, left, backward, right.
Space - Jump; swim upward; fly up (Creative Mode).
Shift - Sneak/crouch; fly down (Creative Mode); prevent falling off edges.
E - Open inventory.
Q - Drop held item.
1-9 / Scroll Wheel - Select hotbar slot.
F5 - Toggle third-person view.
F3 - Debug screen.
