# Description
Minecraft 1.8.8 is a stable release of the legendary sandbox survival game developed by Mojang Studios. Known as "The Bountiful Update," this version introduced ocean monuments, guardians, new stone variants, banners, armor stands, and enhanced command block functionality. Version 1.8 remains highly popular for PvP servers due to its classic combat mechanics featuring fast-paced clicking and no attack cooldown.

# Controls
Left Click - Attack/mine blocks; pick up all items from a slot; split a stack evenly (hold and drag); sort loose items into one stack (double click).
Right Click - Place blocks/use items; pick up half of a stack; place one item from a stack (hold and drag).
WASD - Move forward, left, backward, right.
Space - Jump; swim upward; fly up (Creative Mode).
Shift - Sneak/crouch; fly down (Creative Mode); prevent falling off edges.
E - Open inventory.
Q - Drop held item.
1-9 / Scroll Wheel - Select hotbar slot.
F5 - Toggle third-person view.
F3 - Debug screen.
