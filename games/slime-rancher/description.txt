# Description
[Slime Rancher](https://slimerancher.fandom.com/wiki/Slime_Rancher) is a charming first-person life simulation adventure game developed by [Monomi Park](https://www.monomipark.com/). Players take on the role of Beatrix LeBeau, a young rancher who ventures to the Far, Far Range, a mysterious planet thousands of light-years from Earth. Armed with a vacpack, players collect adorable slimes, raise them on their ranch, harvest valuable plorts, and explore a colorful alien world filled with secrets, treasures, and unique biome zones. With ranch upgrades, gadgets to craft, and rare slimes to discover, Slime Rancher offers a relaxing yet engaging farming adventure.

# Controls
Left Click - Shoot vacpack; launch items.
Right Click - Vacuum; suck up slimes, plorts, and resources.
WASD / ↑←↓→ - Move forward, left, backward, right.
Space - Jump; jetpack (when unlocked).
Shift - Sprint.
Tab - Open vacpack inventory.
1-4 - Select vacpack slot.
Scroll Wheel - Cycle through vacpack slots.
Escape - Pause menu.
M - Open map.
E - Interact; use gadgets.
F1 - Slime index.
